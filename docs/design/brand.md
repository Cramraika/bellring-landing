# Brand — Bellring

## Identity

Bellring is the whitelabel sale-celebration SaaS that brings the sales-floor bell-ringing ritual to distributed sales teams.

## Story

When a deal closes on a sales floor, someone rings a bell. Everyone cheers. The moment is shared. Distributed teams lose this. Bellring restores the ritual: when a sale closes (CRM webhook, manual trigger, browser-extension click), an animated bell rings across every teammate's screen, with a celebratory ripple, optional confetti, and a Slack/Teams broadcast.

## Voice

- **Energetic but professional** — celebratory, never juvenile. Sales-floor pros, not bro-culture.
- **Direct** — sales reps don't have time for fluff. Lead with the result.
- **Inclusive** — every closer gets celebrated, regardless of deal size (configurable thresholds).

### Do
- "Ring the bell. Share the win."
- "Every close. Every teammate. Instantly."
- "Built for closers, by closers."

### Don't
- Generic SaaS phrases ("synergy", "leverage", "best-in-class")
- Frat-bro humor (cringe)
- Underselling — this is a celebration product; the copy should feel like one

## Audience

- **Primary**: B2B SaaS sales teams (5–500 reps), distributed/hybrid
- **Secondary**: real-estate brokerages, agencies, financial-services teams
- **Tertiary**: solopreneur closers (Starter tier)

## Whitelabel angle

Enterprise tier ($299) lets agencies/brokerages reskin Bellring with their own bell sound, logo, palette swap. Brand architecture: Bellring stays the engine, the customer's brand is the surface.

## Pairing

- `bellring-server` (Render.com) — backend API
- `bellring-extension` (Chrome MV3 + Firefox/Edge) — manual-trigger surface
- `bellring-landing` (this repo) — marketing + sign-up

## Domain

`bellring.app` (preferred) or `bellring.io` — operator decides at S11C purchase.
